    Stork (pleroma-bot)¶

    Mirror your favourite Twitter accounts in the Fediverse, so you can follow their updates from the comfort of your own instance.

    Get started Download AUR (Arch)

    Features¶

    • Retrieves tweets and posts them on the Fediverse account
      • Can filter out RTs
      • Can filter out replies
    • Media retrieval and upload of multiple attachments
      • Video
      • Images
      • Animated GIFs
      • Polls
    • Update Fediverse profile info based on the Twitter account
      • Display name
      • Profile picture
      • Banner image
      • Bio text
    • Customize Fediverse account's metadata fields (e.g. point to the original Twitter account)

    Funding¶
